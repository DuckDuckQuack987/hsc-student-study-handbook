Time Management Tips

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 mins, then take a 5 min break. After 4 cycles, take a longer break.

Time Blocking

Assign specific times for each task in your planner or calendar.

Energy Matching

Do harder tasks during your peak energy times (morning/evening).

Buffer Time

Allow 15-30 min gaps between major tasks for unexpected issues.
