Confidence Boosting Techniques

Power Posing

Stand tall with open posture for 2 minutes to reduce stress hormones and boost confidence before big tasks.

Success Journal

Write down three wins every day, no matter how small, to build self-belief over time.

Visualization

Imagine yourself succeeding at your goal. Rehearsing success mentally can help you achieve it in real life.
